Variations & Tips

  • ATMIST: Add Age/Time of injury at the start if your service prefers.
  • Pediatrics: Include weight (kg), Broselow color, and parental consent status.
  • Stroke: Last known well, FAST-ED/NIHSS, glucose, anticoagulants.
  • Sepsis: Suspected source, temp, MAP, lactate (if available), fluids/pressors.
